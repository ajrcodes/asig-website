Zeta Upsilon's History at UVA

Alpha Sigma Phi's Zeta Upsilon Chapter received our charter here at UVA on September 21, 2014, making us one of the youngest and fastest growing chapter on grounds. With the foundation provided by our founding fathers just a few years ago, we continue to improve each year, whether it be through the quality and quantity of our men, philanthropy, housing, academics, etc.

Today...

We have grown to a size of 73 members, featuring a wide array of backgrounds, interests and passions. Our members are involved in UVA groups such as the University Guide Service, ROTC, Alternative Spring Break, Madison House, and many more.

We look forward to continued growth as we strive to empress a truly positive impact on the University Community. Alpha Sigma Phi looks for motivated men who seek brotherhood, leadership opportunities, and potential for personal growth. We are very humbled by our short but meaningful history, as it serves as a constant reminder of both where we came from and where we should be heading.

In the words of Founding Father, Hank Phillips, "It's not a bad life we live"
